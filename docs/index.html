Why it helps.

Environmental information is usually scattered across separate apps and shown as numbers. Dear Environment brings photo, place, weather, air quality, and time into one creative workflow, helping users notice their surroundings and keep meaningful records of everyday environmental moments.

01

Before

02

Problem

Users may check the data quickly, but rarely reflect on what the environment felt like.

03

Solution

The app turns live environmental context into editable postcard material.

04

Result

Users create a personal archive of places, moods, weather, and memories over time.

Not just generated. Personalised.

Dear Environment is built around interaction. The generated data becomes editable material: templates, stickers, captions, image position, and final sharing choices.

Switch visual templates
Add, drag, scale, and delete stickers
Move and scale the photo
Change or copy generated captions
Export PNG or share through the native share sheet

Built for Connected Environments.

The project links the user, phone sensors, external environmental APIs, and creative interaction. Environmental data becomes something visible, editable, and memorable.

Device

Camera, GPS, touch gestures, gallery access, local storage, and native sharing.

APIs

Open-Meteo weather, Open-Meteo air quality, reverse geocoding, and optional LLM endpoint.

Output

Rendered PNG postcards, generated captions, and saved local Album entries.

Testing

Passed flutter analyze and flutter test, with manual testing across the main app flow.
